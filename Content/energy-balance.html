charge conservation:

\[\begin{split} & \frac{\partial}{\partial t}\varrho + \nabla \cdot j = 0 \end{split} \tag{1}\]

\(\varrho = \sigma\delta = \varepsilon_0 \nabla\cdot \mathscr{E} =\) charge density in interior

\(\sigma =\) charge density at border (skin)

\(\delta =\) Dirac delta-function

\(\boldsymbol{j} = \sigma \left( \mathscr{E} + v \times \mathscr{B} \right) = \nabla \times \mathscr{H} - \frac{\partial}{\partial t}\mathscr{D} = \frac{1}{\mu_0}\nabla\times\mathscr{B} - \varepsilon_0\frac{\partial}{\partial t}\mathscr{E} \hat{=}\) elecectric current density

\(\boldsymbol{j} \cdot \mathscr{E} \hat{=}\) energy gain by fields \(\mathscr{E}\) and \(\mathscr{B}\) through current density \(j\)

\(\mathscr{S} = \mathscr{E} \times \mathscr{H} \hat{=}\) energy transport density (Intensity = \(\left| \overline{\mathscr{S}} \right|\))

\[\begin{split} & \int \left( \frac{\partial}{\partial t} \left( w_e + w_m \right) + \mathbf{\nabla} \cdot \mathbf{\mathscr{S}} + \boldsymbol{j} \cdot \mathscr{E} \right) \, dr = \frac{d}{dt}W_e + \frac{d}{dt}W_m + D_S + \int \left( \boldsymbol{j} \cdot \mathscr{E} \right) \, dr \newline & \stackrel{\textnormal{low frequency}}{=} \frac{1}{2}\frac{d}{dt} \left( LI^2 \right) + \frac{1}{2}\frac{d}{dt} \left( \frac{Q^2}{C} \right) + \left( RI^2 - VI \right) = 0 \end{split} \tag{2}\]

Energy Balance

Electro-Magnetic Energy: \[\begin{split} & E = E_{kin} + E_{pot} = \sqrt{ \left( pc \right)^2 + \left( mc^2 \right)^2 } \stackrel{\text{vacuum}}{=} pc = \hbar\omega \newline & = \text{chargeenergy + fieldenergy + transportenergy} \end{split} \tag{3}\]

Charge Energy: \[\begin{split} & \underbrace{ j \cdot \mathscr{E} }_\text{charge energy} = - \left( \underbrace{ \frac{\partial}{\partial t} \sum w_i }_\text{field energy} + \underbrace{ \nabla\cdot\mathscr{S} }_\text{transfer energy} \right) = \mathscr{E} \cdot \nabla\times\mathscr{A} - \mathscr{E} \cdot \frac{\partial}{\partial t}\mathscr{D} \end{split} \tag{4}\]

Field Energy: \[\begin{split} & \frac{\partial}{\partial t} w = \frac{\partial}{\partial t} \sum w_i = \frac{\partial}{\partial t} \left( w_e + w_m \right) = \frac{\partial}{\partial t} \left( \frac{\varepsilon_0}{2}\mathscr{E}^2 + \frac{1}{2\mu_0}\mathscr{B}^2 \right) \stackrel{vacuum}{=} \frac{\partial}{\partial t} \left( \frac{1}{2}\frac{1}{\mu_0}\left( \mathscr{A}\cdot\nabla\times\mathscr{B} - \mathscr{B}^2 \right) \right) \end{split} \tag{5}\]

Transfer Energy: \[\begin{split} & - \dot{w} \, \hat{=} \, \nabla \cdot \mathscr{S} \stackrel{matter}{=} \nabla \cdot \left( \mathscr{E} \times \mathscr{H} \right) \stackrel{vacuum}{=} \nabla \cdot \left( \mathscr{E} \times \mathscr{B} \right) \stackrel{\text{alternativ in vacuum}}{=} \nabla \cdot \left( \frac{1}{2}\frac{1}{\mu_0}\frac{\partial}{\partial t} \left( \mathscr{A}\times\mathscr{B} \right) \right) = ... \end{split} \tag{6}\]

Tension Force: Stress-Tensor

Stress Tensor quantifies tension \(\hat{=} \Delta\) Forces = Pressure - Traction \(\hat{=}\) “torsion tension” at angular momentum

\[\begin{split} & \mathscr{T} \hat{=} \mathscr{T}_{ik} = \delta_{ik} \left( \frac{1}{2}\varepsilon_0\mathscr{E}^2 + \frac{1}{2}\frac{1}{\mu_0}\mathscr{B}^2 \right) - \varepsilon_0\mathscr{E}_i\mathscr{E}_k - \frac{1}{\mu_0}\mathscr{B}_i\mathscr{B}_k \end{split} \tag{13}\]

\(\mathscr{E}\) and \(\mathscr{B}\) fields of spacial confined charge and current density distribution, far field, static: \(\mathscr{T}\sim \frac{1}{r^4}\) \(\Leftrightarrow\) \(\int_{\mathbb{R}^3}\mathscr{T}d^3r = \int_{\infty}\mathscr{T}df = 0\)
